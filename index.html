About Me

Madhu Lakha is a seasoned hair and makeup artist located in Northern California, with over a decade of experience. She has skillfully catered to a diverse clientele across Sacramento, the Bay Area, and neighboring cities. Despite her proficiency in her field, Madhu remains dedicated to continuous learning, she has completed a course with Blue Rose Artistry and obtained certifications from Beauty Innovations and Hair by Shak.
